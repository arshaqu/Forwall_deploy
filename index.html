About Us

Fortwall International Contracting Company

The FORTWALL Company consists of full engineering discipline to provide a solution to various industries according to its technical structure. The company has a dynamic approach towards the refinement of necessities to give ultimate results beneficial for parties involved. The company is consistently striving to be the first choice among so many, within the ambit of its resources, capability, technical structure, and instantly available resources. Our team comprising of different fields of knowledge includes civil, mechanical and electrical engineers with magnifying careers. The team is fully equipped in respect of all inevitable to perform small and medium-sized projects.

Fortwall has diversified experience in various fields of construction activities. Fortwall is dynamic in these sectors i.e. civil, mechanical and electrical; however the company’s main expertise lies in the sphere of civil construction.

FORTWALL, in short, provides a full range of technical sound support services in industrial and commercial construction activities with the ultimate goal to satisfy our clients, eventually to maximize the profits of the parties concerned. The company has it’s Head Office located in Al Khobar and sub-office in Riyadh and branches in India , UAE and Bahrain.
